Main Components of your favorite coffee drinks

Coffee

You may obtain a delicious cup of coffee in many ways. A major figure in pop culture is the Chemex. Some others are french press, Aeropress, V60 and many more. Drinking a delicious cup of coffee just when you wake up has never been more easy.

Espresso

Sometimes you want that shot of pure caffeine. Espresso is the name of your drink. Coffee beans are grinded to a very fine consistency and water is passed through a compressed puck of coffee grinds at a high pressure getting a drink that will wake you right up.

Milk

Milk makes your coffee more mellow by cutting through the natural bitterness of coffee. Steaming milk and then introducing plenty of air into it gives you foam. Milk is the main component of some of your favorite drinks like a Cappuccino or Lattes
